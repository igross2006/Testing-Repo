# 🐉 Mythlings

Cute mythical pocket pets that live in your phone. Hatch mystery eggs, cuddle
your creatures, catch their giggles, and build them a cozy home.

## The creatures

| | Name | Species |
|---|------|---------|
| 🐉 | **Sprout** | Baby dragon — hoards shiny things, sneezes smoke rings |
| 🔥 | **Cinder** | Pocket phoenix — extremely dramatic, will NOT combust (probably) |
| 🐙 | **Inky** | Bathtub kraken — gives four-arm hugs, collects forks |

## How to play

- **Tap the egg** to hatch your first Mythling.
- **Stroke your pet** with your finger to pet it (hearts! purring!).
- **Feed** 🍪 and **tuck them in** 🌙 to keep their stats up.
- Happy pets shed **giggle sparkles ✨** — tap to collect them.
- Sleeping pets make **dream bubbles 💭** — pop them for bonus giggles.
- Spend giggles on **new eggs** (up to 3 pets) and **new homes** 🏞️.
- Pets left hungry don't die — they just get *theatrical* about it.

## Environments

- 🌤️ **Cloud Meadow** — free starter home
- 🌙 **Starfall Grove** — stars and fireflies (100 ✨)
- 🌊 **Tidepool Cove** — waves and warm sand (100 ✨)

The scene dims to real night when it's nighttime where you are.

## Play it on your phone

1. Enable GitHub Pages: repo **Settings → Pages → Deploy from a branch**,
   pick this branch, folder `/ (root)`, save.
2. Open the Pages URL on your phone.
3. **Add to Home Screen** for the full app feel (works offline after first load).

Progress saves automatically on your device (localStorage).
